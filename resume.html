Professional Summary

Manufacturing and process engineer with experience across sheet metal design, fabrication and ERP-driven production in India, the UAE and the UK since 2017. Today I work inside a live precision sheet metal manufacturing environment, with a dual focus on production engineering and the software systems that run the floor. Over 2025 I led a digital transformation programme that moved core operations from paper to a substantially digitised, ERP-integrated production environment — specifying, designing, validating and deploying four systems — built using AI-assisted software development under my own engineering direction — now in active daily use across 19 operators and 14 work centres.

The work spans the full path from operational problem to deployed system: shop floor digitisation, real-time production intelligence, and the automation of engineering document and job pack workflows — all integrated against MIE Trak ERP on factory infrastructure. Every requirement was identified through direct operational involvement, and I continue to maintain and improve each system in daily production use.
